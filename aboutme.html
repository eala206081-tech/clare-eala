Performing Arts

I've always had a passion for music. I'm almost entirely self taught in guitar, ukulele, and voice and have had experience performing in front of large audiences


In elementary, I was a member of the choir at my local church. In Junior High, I became chorale mayor of the school choir and performed under the guidance of venerated chorale masters such as Sir Michael De Leon and Sir Isaac Iglesias. Continued my streak and became unofficial head of the TAMMS Chorale of FEU Alabang and performed for Christmas specials at Crimson Hotel

Media Arts

I have a deep love for classical and digital arts. I learned to paint by watching Bob Ross tutorials growing up and built my technique from various online resources. I was fortunate enough to take a Fernando Amorsolo painting class last year to further hone my skills in acrylic painting. I also dabble in a bit of digital arts, especially for academic and extracurricular purposes.
